Our Story

Where Pure Veg Meets
Great Taste

Born from a passion for pure, honest food, CAD M CAD B – Younger's Hunger has become Durg's go-to destination for vegetarians who refuse to compromise on taste or quality. Nestled on Gurudwara Road, we serve everything from lip-smacking Chinese starters and creamy pasta to sizzling brownies and signature thick shakes.

We believe that vegetarian food can be exciting, indulgent, and deeply satisfying. Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients, cooked with care, and served with warmth. Whether it's a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a birthday celebration — we make every meal memorable.
